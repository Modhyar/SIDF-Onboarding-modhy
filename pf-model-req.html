Note: There are two types: Consolidated and Standalone. Unlike the consolidated model, a standalone model does not require historical financials.
Credit

Credit Requirements

Inputs mainly from financial statements and credit analysis.

  • Income Statement and Balance Sheet
  • Cash conversion cycle
  • SG&A percentage (note: subtract Depreciation & Amortization)
  • PP&E (for the most recent year)
  • Historical financials (only for consolidated)
Market

Market Requirements

From (FMR) Full Market Report.

  • Product line
  • Selling price
  • Raw material cost
  • Volume
Technical

Technical Requirements

From (FTR) Full Technical Report.

  • Indirect & direct salary
  • Utilities
  • Capital expenditure, depreciation, amortization, and maintenance rate
