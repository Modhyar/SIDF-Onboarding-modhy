Department Introduction

Relationship Management (RM) supports industrial clients through project evaluation, credit analysis, and coordination across internal stakeholders.

What RM (Credit) does

  • Evaluate requests (financial + business understanding)
  • Build credit views using ratios, trends, cash flow, risks
  • Support decision making with structured reports (e.g., PER style)
  • Coordinate with other departments for complete assessment
